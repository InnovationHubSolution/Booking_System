## 2. Prerequisites

Before starting the onboarding process, ensure you have:

### Personal Requirements
- [ ] **Age**: 18 years or older
- [ ] **Legal Status**: Legal right to operate a business in Vanuatu
- [ ] **Bank Account**: Valid bank account for receiving payments
- [ ] **Contact Information**: Valid email and phone number

### Business Requirements (if applicable)
- [ ] **Business Registration**: Valid business license (if operating as a business)
- [ ] **Tourism License**: Vanuatu Tourism Registration (for accommodation/tours)
- [ ] **Tax Registration**: Vanuatu Tax ID (TIN)
- [ ] **Insurance**: Public liability insurance (recommended, may be required)

### Property/Service Requirements
- [ ] **Ownership or Authorization**: Proof of property ownership or permission to list
- [ ] **Safety Standards**: Compliance with local safety regulations
- [ ] **Quality Photos**: High-quality images of your property/service
- [ ] **Accurate Information**: Complete and accurate listing details

## 10. FAQs

### General Questions

**Q: How much does it cost to become a host?**
A: It's FREE to create a host account and list your property. We only charge a commission (12%) when you receive a booking.

**Q: How long does the onboarding process take?**
A: Typically 3-5 business days from application submission to going live, depending on how quickly you complete each step.

**Q: Can I list multiple properties?**
A: Yes! Once approved, you can add unlimited listings from your dashboard.

**Q: Do I need a business license?**
A: It depends. If you're hosting occasionally, you may not need one. For regular hosting or multiple properties, a business license is required. Check with Vanuatu Business License Office.

**Q: What if I don't own the property?**
A: You can still list it if you have written permission from the owner. Upload the authorization letter during document submission.

### Financial Questions

**Q: When do I get paid?**
A: Funds are released 24 hours after the guest checks in. Payouts are processed within 1-2 business days.

**Q: Can I set my own prices?**
A: Absolutely! You have full control over your pricing. We provide market insights to help you price competitively.

**Q: What happens if a guest cancels?**
A: Depends on your cancellation policy and when they cancel. You'll receive a portion or full payment based on your policy.

**Q: Are there any hidden fees?**
A: No. Our fees are transparent: 12% platform fee + 2.9% + 30Â¢ payment processing fee.

### Operational Questions

**Q: What if I'm not available for check-in?**
A: You can arrange third-party check-in, use a lockbox/keypad, or designate someone to handle it.

**Q: Can I decline a booking request?**
A: Yes, but frequent declines lower your ranking. It's better to keep your calendar updated to avoid requests when unavailable.

**Q: What if there's an emergency and I need to cancel?**
A: Contact support immediately. Emergency cancellations (natural disasters, serious illness) are handled case-by-case without penalty.

**Q: How do I handle guest complaints?**
A: Address issues promptly and professionally. Most concerns can be resolved with good communication. Contact support if needed.

### Platform Questions

**Q: Is my personal information safe?**
A: Yes. We use bank-level encryption and never share your personal details with guests until after booking confirmation.

**Q: Can I use this platform if I'm also on Airbnb/Booking.com?**
A: Yes! Just ensure your calendar is synced to avoid double bookings. We support iCal integration.

**Q: How visible will my listing be?**
A: New listings get a visibility boost for the first 30 days. After that, rankings depend on reviews, response rate, and booking performance.

**Q: What if I have technical issues?**
A: Contact our support team anytime via chat, email, or phone. We're here to help!
